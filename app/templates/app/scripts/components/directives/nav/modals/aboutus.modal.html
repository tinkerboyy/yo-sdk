The Acquisition Gateway is managed by GSA’s Common Acquisition Platform (CAP) team.  Using the practice of frequent, iterative development we let customer feedback inform how and what we build.  

  • GSA’s Common Acquisition Platform (CAP) team is building the Acquisition Gateway to be the hub of an acquisition professional’s daily activities.  We are revamping how the government approaches procurement, replacing the decades-old model of a fragmented and scattered approach to acquisition.   Join us and Act as One!
     

  • We are a working example of the government’s adoption of Agile. The CAP team follows the principles of frequent iterative feedback from across government as we build the Acquisition Gateway using a collaborative and transparent process.

  • We connect professionals who want to share advice, success stories, and lessons learned.  Join the conversation today! Interact
